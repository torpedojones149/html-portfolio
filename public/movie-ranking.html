My favorite movies at this time

Top 3 movies

Spirited Away

This is my favorite anime movie. It's beautifully animated, has an amazing soundtrack and the story is very compelling like most Studio Ghibli/Miyazaki films.

Me, Myself and Irene

Hilarious Jim Carrey movie that doesn't seem to get a lot of credit. Also, I really feel-good movie for me with a great soundtrack.

Lord of the Rings trilogy (extended edition)

This one is kind of a copout because it's three movies but I feel like all three of the LOTR movies are really amazing and it's hard to see one without seeing them all together.
